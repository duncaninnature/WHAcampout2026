We Have Arrived Summer Campout 2026

Group camping weekend at Sanborn County Park

Important: Registration is not handled through this page; please contact Duncan to register.
Slots reserved: 3 / 35

Main Info

  • Dates: June 12–14, 2026
  • Check-in: 3:00 PM Friday
  • Check-out: 1:00 PM Sunday
  • Registration: $60

Registration includes the group site fee/food for the weekend. Your spot is officially reserved once payment is received.

Please bring a tent, sleeping bag, snacks, and other essentials for camping in the woods over the weekend. If you do not have certain essential supplies, please let Duncan know by May 4th and he will help source.

Site / Area Details

This is a hike-in group campsite. The paved path leading to the site is about half a mile long and mostly uphill. Wheelbarrow carts will be available for shuttling supplies to and from the site.

Parking for 6 vehicles in the overflow lot is included with the reservation. Larger vehicles will be given parking pass priority for the sake of efficiency with carpooling and materials transport.

Directions: Sanborn County Park is located on Sanborn Road. From downtown Saratoga, proceed two miles west on Highway 9 to Sanborn Road. Turn left onto Sanborn Road and travel one mile to the park entrance.

Campground Rules & Agreement

View regulations
  • Quiet hours are from 10:00 PM to 7:00 AM.
  • Radios, instruments, and other loud noise are not permitted during quiet hours.
  • Shower facilities are available at each camping park.
  • Showers are on timers and are only for those with a camping reservation.
  • Dogs and pets are allowed in all campgrounds.
  • Pets must be on a leash no longer than 6 feet and under the owner's control at all times.
  • Pets must be confined to tents or vehicles at night.
  • Maximum of 2 pets per site.
  • No wood gathering is permitted.
  • No fires are permitted on the ground.
  • Charcoal is permitted in grills, BBQ pits, and fire rings.
  • Wood fires are not permitted in the large BBQ grills at reserved sites.
  • Fires in BBQ pits must be kept beneath the grate.
  • Please allow fires in grills, BBQ pits, and fire rings to burn out naturally.
  • Open fires and charcoal use may be restricted during high fire danger periods.
  • Camp stoves and similar devices with a controlled enclosed flame are allowed.
  • General setup and cleanup are the responsibility of the group.
  • Buildings and grounds must be left free of debris and other refuse.
  • Clean fire rings and empty refuse and glass.
  • All decorations and equipment must be removed from the site.
  • Additional cleanup may be charged at the special staffing fee rate.
  • Future reservations may be denied until past fees are paid.
  • Glitter and confetti are not allowed.
  • Staples are not permitted on picnic tables, wood structures, or trees.
  • Hammocks must be free-standing.
  • Swimming is prohibited in all County parks.
  • Smoking is prohibited in all County parks.
