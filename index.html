We Have Arrived 2026 Campout Info Sheet

Sanborn County Park

Important: This page is for information only. Registration is handled separately and not through this page.

Main Info

  • Dates: June 12–14, 2026
  • Check-in: 3:00 PM Friday
  • Check-out: 1:00 PM Sunday
  • Registration: $60

Registration includes the group site fee and food for the weekend. A spot is reserved once payment is received.

Bring a tent, sleeping bag, snacks, and essentials. If you need gear, please coordinate ahead of time.

Site Details

This is a hike-in campsite (~0.5 mile uphill). Wheelbarrow carts will be available.

Parking for 6 vehicles is included. Larger vehicles are prioritized for hauling.

Directions: From Saratoga, take Highway 9 west → turn left onto Sanborn Road → continue 1 mile to the entrance.

Campground Rules & Agreement

View regulations
  • Quiet hours are from 10:00 PM to 7:00 AM.
  • No loud noise during quiet hours.
  • Pets allowed but must be leashed and controlled.
  • No wood gathering.
  • No ground fires.
  • Charcoal allowed in grills only.
  • Camp stoves permitted.
  • Clean up all debris and remove all equipment.
  • No smoking in County parks.
